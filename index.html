The Problem

Your machine is powerful.
The report it produces isn't.

DEXA scans capture some of the most useful clinical data in bariatric medicine. The standard software just doesn't do anything meaningful with it.

01
Reports patients can't understand

The standard DEXA output is four pages of numbers and reference curves. Patients leave confused. Clinicians spend the visit explaining instead of deciding.

02
Data you can't act on

Every scan lives in its own PDF. There's no way to see whether a patient is trending in the right direction between visits without pulling old reports and comparing manually.

03
No longitudinal view

Bariatric outcomes are measured over months and years. Without a unified patient timeline, you're flying blind on whether treatment is actually working.

How It Works

Three steps from scan
to clinical decision.

01
Export from your DEXA machine

Staff exports the scan file from your DEXA machine and drops it in a shared folder. No reformatting, no manual entry — just the file as-is.

02
Basil processes the data

Basil extracts body composition, bone density, and fracture risk data — normalizes it against clinical reference populations, and builds a structured patient profile.

03
Dashboard + reports

Your roster updates instantly. Risk-flagged patients surface to the top. Clinical PDF reports — ready to share with patients or referring physicians — are generated automatically.
